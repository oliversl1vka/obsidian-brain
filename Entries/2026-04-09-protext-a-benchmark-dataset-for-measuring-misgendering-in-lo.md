## [2026-04-09] ProText: A Benchmark Dataset for Measuring (Mis)gendering in Long-Form Texts

**Context:** Understanding and mitigating gender bias in LLMs is essential for building fairer NLP tools aligned with inclusive AI goals and relevant to my interests in LLM fine tuning and automated language tasks.
**Insight:** ProText provides a nuanced benchmark dataset that detects (mis)gendering beyond binary pronoun resolution by categorizing diverse theme nouns and pronouns, revealing systemic gender biases in LLM outputs especially when gender cues are ambiguous or defaulted to heteronormative norms.
**Application:** Use ProText to evaluate and improve LLM performance on gender bias and fairness in text generation, summarization, and translation tasks to create more inclusive AI-powered language models.
