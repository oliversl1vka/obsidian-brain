## [2026-04-09] Evaluating alignment of behavioral dispositions in LLMs

**Context:** Understanding and improving LLM alignment with human behavior is critical for reliable AI advisory tools, aligning with my focus on fine tuning and behavioral evaluation of models.
**Insight:** Google Research developed a novel framework converting psychological questionnaires into situational judgment tests to evaluate LLM behavioral alignment, revealing that larger models align better with human consensus in clear cases but show overconfidence and lack diversity awareness in ambiguous cases, highlighting gaps in both directional and distributional alignment.
**Application:** Apply this evaluation framework to assess and guide fine tuning or training efforts aimed at improving LLM behavioral alignment, especially for socially sensitive applications.
