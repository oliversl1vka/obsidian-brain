## [2026-04-09] Measuring progress toward AGI: A cognitive framework

**Context:** Understanding rigorous frameworks for AGI measurement is crucial for tracking progress and benchmarking capabilities in AI projects, especially when fine-tuning and evaluating models.
**Insight:** The paper defines 10 essential cognitive abilities for AGI assessment and proposes a three-stage method benchmarking AI against human performance, with community-driven challenges focusing on under-measured skills like metacognition and social cognition to create scalable evaluations.
**Application:** Leverage this framework and community benchmarks to systematically evaluate and track cognitive abilities of AI models during development and fine-tuning for broad generalization.
