## [2026-04-09] Personalized Group Relative Policy Optimization for Heterogenous Preference Alignment

**Context:** Aligning LLMs with diverse user preferences is key for creating personalized, effective AI models aligned with my fine-tuning and model training goals.
**Insight:** Personalized Group Relative Policy Optimization (P-GRPO) improves on standard GRPO by normalizing advantage signals within preference-specific groups rather than across all data, preserving minority preferences and enabling faster convergence with higher rewards in reinforcement learning for LLMs.
**Application:** Apply P-GRPO when fine-tuning LLMs to align heterogeneous human preferences efficiently during reinforcement learning, enhancing personalized model behavior without sacrificing general performance.
