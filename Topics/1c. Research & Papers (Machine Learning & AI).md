### [2026-04-09] Evaluating alignment of behavioral dispositions in LLMs
**Insight:** Google Research developed a novel framework converting psychological questionnaires into situational judgment tests to evaluate LLM behavioral alignment, revealing that larger models align better with human consensus in clear cases but show overconfidence and lack diversity awareness in ambiguous cases, highlighting gaps in both directional and distributional alignment.
**Source:** [Evaluating alignment of behavioral dispositions in LLMs](https://research.google/blog/evaluating-alignment-of-behavioral-dispositions-in-llms/) · [[Entries/2026-04-09-evaluating-alignment-of-behavioral-dispositions-in-llms]]

### [2026-04-09] ProText: A Benchmark Dataset for Measuring (Mis)gendering in Long-Form Texts
**Insight:** ProText provides a nuanced benchmark dataset that detects (mis)gendering beyond binary pronoun resolution by categorizing diverse theme nouns and pronouns, revealing systemic gender biases in LLM outputs especially when gender cues are ambiguous or defaulted to heteronormative norms.
